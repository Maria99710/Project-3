ZIYU WANG

A Graphic designer, Film learner, a student and a dreamer in Vancouver

Hi, all! I’m currently a fourth-year student in SFU. The interest in graphic design and filming brings me to SIAT. I love all beautiful objects in nature and they always can bring me inspiration. Design is not only a work, but also comes into my life, and my goal is to bring design back to nature and life to make our like beautiful, also communicate the message to the people through visual language.

​

I can skillfully operate some design editing software such as Photoshop, AI, ID, AE, PR, Maya. I would like to shoot some VLOG about my daily life and edit the short videos to post it. I have a great interest in shooting and cutting videos, and knowledge of graphic design is indispensable. I hope to be able to work in the entertainment service industry in the future, integrate my hobbies into my work, and hope that I can start my own business and do what I like while serving everyone.
